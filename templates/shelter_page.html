Marina Animal Shelter

Marina Animal Shelter is an all-volunteer, non-profit, animal rescue based in Marina, CA. Our mission is to relieve overcrowded animal shelters through rescue, thereby reducing euthanasia. Formed in 2017, Marina Animal Shelter starts Animal Shelter Relief rescue, the ONLY local animal rescue all kinds of animals, including chickens and turtles. The founders know what help is needed at the County Animal Shelter and formed a rescue to meet those needs and relieve the overcrowding and subsequent euthanasia of otherwise adoptable animals.
